Orbit Wander

A portrait space puzzle game built around gravity wells, orbit capture, and short dash decisions. Hold to aim, release to launch, and use each planet's pull to reach the gate with enough stars and few enough dashes.

  • 50 staged level goals
  • One-touch aiming
  • Stars, dash targets, and local records
  • Offline play

Gameplay

Orbit Wander is not a tile swap or match puzzle. Each run is a vertical route through space. Planets bend the probe's path, safe gravity wells can catch it into orbit, and the next dash decides whether it reaches the gate, collects stars, or drifts out of the flight path.

The level score is based on distance, collected stars, and dash efficiency. Later levels introduce higher target distances, tighter dash goals, hazardous planets, moving asteroids, and shield crystals.
