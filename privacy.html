Orbit Wander Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026

Orbit Wander is a casual space puzzle game. This policy describes the current version of the app with local gameplay progress, security protection using a device identifier, and third-party rewarded advertising that is initialized after the App Tracking Transparency permission flow where required.

Data We Collect

Orbit Wander does not ask for a name, email address, phone number, account login, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone access, or camera access. The app uses local gameplay data, device and security identifiers, a backend advertising configuration request, and third-party advertising services as described below.

Local Game Data

The app stores gameplay progress locally on the device, including unlocked levels, star ratings, best distance, score, and audio or trajectory settings. This local gameplay data is used to continue gameplay and maintain in-game settings.

Security and Device Identifiers

On launch, the app may use the device's Identifier for Vendor (IDFV) as a security user identifier for HSSecurity. This is used for fraud and abuse prevention, app integrity, and service stability. The security SDK may process technical information such as device, app, network, and diagnostic signals for those purposes.

The app also requests ad unit configuration from https://ad-call.de123.net/app/ad/units using the app bundle identifier. This request is used to decide which advertising platforms and ad units are enabled for the current app version.

Tracking and Advertising

Orbit Wander may display rewarded ads through Chartboost, Yandex, Vungle, and InMobi. These providers are initialized only when the app receives valid dynamic advertising configuration for that provider. Depending on the player's consent choices and the ad partner's configuration, these services may process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from network information, app interaction events, ad impressions, and diagnostic data.

On iOS, if tracking permission is required, the app presents Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before allowing access to the device advertising identifier for personalized advertising. If the player does not allow tracking, the app will not use the advertising identifier for tracking, although ads may still be shown in a contextual or limited-personalization form.

Children's Privacy

Orbit Wander does not knowingly ask children to provide personal information. The app does not include account profiles or user-submitted content. Advertising partners may apply their own child-directed treatment, limited ads, or consent rules where required.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information directly. Advertising partners and the security SDK provider may receive or process technical, security, and advertising-related information to provide, protect, measure, limit, and improve their services, subject to the player's consent choices and applicable privacy settings.

Changes to This Policy

If a future version changes the advertising partners, security SDK behavior, analytics, cloud saves, account features, or other data collection practices, this policy and the App Store privacy labels will be updated before that version is released.
