Orbit Wander Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Orbit Wander is a casual puzzle game. This policy describes the current version of the app with local gameplay progress and third-party advertising shown after the App Tracking Transparency permission flow where required.

Data We Collect

Orbit Wander does not ask for a name, email address, phone number, account login, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone access, or camera access. The app may use third-party advertising services that process technical and advertising-related information as described below.

Local Game Data

The app stores gameplay progress locally on the device, including unlocked levels, star ratings, best distance, score, and audio or trajectory settings. This local gameplay data is used to continue gameplay and maintain in-game settings.

Tracking and Advertising

Orbit Wander uses third-party advertising services to display ads. Depending on the player's consent choices and the ad partner's configuration, these services may process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from network information, app interaction events, ad impressions, and diagnostic data.

On iOS, if tracking permission is required, the app presents Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before allowing access to the device advertising identifier for personalized advertising. If the player does not allow tracking, the app will not use the advertising identifier for tracking, although ads may still be shown in a contextual or limited-personalization form.

Children's Privacy

Orbit Wander does not knowingly ask children to provide personal information. The app does not include account profiles or user-submitted content. Advertising partners may apply their own child-directed treatment, limited ads, or consent rules where required.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information directly. Advertising partners may receive or process advertising-related information to provide, measure, limit, and improve ads, subject to the player's consent choices and applicable privacy settings.

Changes to This Policy

If a future version changes the advertising partners, adds analytics, cloud saves, account features, or changes data collection practices, this policy and the App Store privacy labels will be updated before that version is released.
