Orbit Wander Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Orbit Wander is an offline casual puzzle game. This policy describes the current version of the app as submitted without account features, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or online services.

Data We Collect

Orbit Wander does not collect personal information from players. The app does not ask for a name, email address, phone number, account login, location, contacts, photos, microphone access, or camera access.

Local Game Data

The app stores gameplay progress locally on the device, including unlocked levels, star ratings, best distance, score, and audio or trajectory settings. This data stays on the device and is used only to continue gameplay.

Tracking and Advertising

The current version does not use third-party advertising, tracking, or analytics services. It does not request permission to track the player across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Children's Privacy

Orbit Wander does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no account profile or server-side player record to delete.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information because the app does not collect personal information.

Changes to This Policy

If a future version adds online services, advertising, analytics, cloud saves, or any other data collection, this policy and the App Store privacy labels will be updated before that version is released.

Contact
